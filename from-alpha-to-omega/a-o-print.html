Verbs

ω-Verbs: Present Active Indicative, Infinitive, and Imperative

Present "release" Τranslation Ending: λυ + ο/ε
S. 1 I release
S. 2 You (s) release
S. 3 He/She/It releases
P. 1 We release
P. 2 You (pl) release
P. 3 They release
Inf. To release
Imperative:
S. 2 Release!
S. 3 Let him release!
P. 2 Release!
P. 3 Let them release!

ω-Verbs: Future Active Indicative and Infinitive

Present "release" Τranslation Ending: λυ + ο/ε
S. 1 I will release
S. 2 You (s) will release
S. 3 He/She/It will release
P. 1 We will release
P. 2 You (pl) will release
P. 3 They will release
Inf. To release

ω-Verbs: Imperfect Active Indicative

Imperfect "release" Translation Ending: ἐ + λυ + ο/ε
S. 1 I was releasing
S. 2 You (s) were releasing
S. 3 He/She/It was releasing
P. 1 We were releasing
P. 2 You (pl) were releasing
P. 3 They were releasing

μι-verbs: εἰμί

Present Active Indicative Translation
S. 1 I am
S. 2 You (s) are
S. 3 He/She/It is
P. 1 We are
P. 2 You (pl) are
P. 3 They are
Inf. To be
Imperative:
S. 2 Be!
S. 3 Let him/her/it be!
P. 2 Be!
P. 3 Let them be!
Imperfect Active Indicative Translation
S. 1 I was
S. 2 You (s) were
S. 3 He/She/It was
P. 1 We were
P. 2 You (pl) were
P. 3 They were
Future Middle Indicative Translation
S. 1 I will be
S. 2 You (s) will be
S. 3 He/She/It will be
P. 1 We will be
P. 2 You (pl) will be
P. 3 They will be
Inf. To be going to be

Prepositions: Governing Cases

Preposition Case Governed Translation
ὑπό (3) + 'by/with/under'
εἰς + 'into/to'
ἐκ (ἐξ) + 'out of/from'
ἐν + 'in'
διά (δι') (2) + 'through/on account of'
