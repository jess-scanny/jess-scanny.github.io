1St Declension: Types

The ε, ι, ρ Rule

IF: the stem ends in ε, ι, or ρ
THEN: use ᾱ instead of η in the singular

  • 1st declension nouns:
    • Long vowel (ᾱ/η)
    • Short vowel (α)
    • Masculine

Verbs

ω-Verbs: Present Active Indicative, Infinitive, and Imperative

Present "release" Τranslation Ending: λυ + ο/ε
S. 1 λυω I release -ω
S. 2 λυεις You (s) release -εις
S. 3 λυει He/She/It releases -ει
P. 1 λυομεν We release -ομεν
P. 2 λυετε You (pl) release -ετε
P. 3 λυουσι(ν) They release -ουσι(ν)
Inf. λυειν To release -ειν
Imperative:
S. 2 λυε Release! -ε
S. 3 λυετω Let him release! -ετω
P. 2 λυετε Release! -ετε
P. 3 λυοντων Let them release! -οντων
Present "send" Translation Ending: πεμπ + ο/ε
S. 1 πεμπω I send -ω
S. 2 πεμπεις You (s) send -εις
S. 3 πεμπει He/She/It sends -ει
P. 1 πεμπομεν We send -ομεν
P. 2 πεμπετε You (pl) send -ετε
P. 3 πεμπουσι(ν) They send -ουσι(ν)
Inf. πεμπειν To Send -ειν
Imperative:
S. 2 πεμπε Send! -ε
S. 3 πεμπετω Let him send! -ετω
P. 2 πεμπετε Send! -ετε
P. 3 πεμποντων Let them send! -οντων

μι-verbs: εἰμί

Present Active Indicative Translation
S. 1 εἰμί I am
S. 2 εἶ You (s) are
S. 3 ἐστί(ν) He/She/It is
P. 1 ἐσμέν We are
P. 2 ἐστέ You (pl) are
P. 3 εἰσί(ν) They are
Inf. εἶναι To be
Imperative:
S. 2 ἴσθι Be!
S. 3 ἔστω Let him/her/it be!
P. 2 ἔστε Be!
P. 3 ἔστων Let them be!
Imperfect Active Indicative Translation
S. 1 ἦ / ἦν I was
S. 2 ἦσθα You (s) were
S. 3 ἦν He/She/It was
P. 1 ἦμεν We were
P. 2 ἦτε You (pl) were
P. 3 ἦσαν They were
