Sweet Potato and Spinach Daal

makes ~3 tupperwares, lasts 5/6 days in the fridge, can be frozen

Ingredients

General

  • 3x red onions (4 small ones)
  • 2 heads garlic
  • 250g lentils
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin coconut milk
  • 5x frozen spinach balls
  • sweet potatoes (roughly 5 small ones, 1 very full bowl when chopped)
  • some oil
  • 1 cube vegetable stock
  • some lemon juice
  • lots salt
  • lots pepper

Spices

(numbers very roughly equate to tablespoons)
  • curry powder 3
  • garam masala 2
  • tumeric 3
  • ginger 1
  • paprika 1
  • cumin 2
  • coriander 2
  • chili 2
  • cinnamon1

Steps

  • add oil and onions (heat 3, lid on)
leave 5 mins
  • drop heat to 2 and chop garlic, potatoes
  • add garlic and pepper
leave 5 mins
  • add coconut milk, vegetable stock (use coconut milk can to measure)
  • add 250g/half bag of lentils and stir (heat up to 4, lid on)
leave ~5 mins
  • add sweet potatoes (or soften seperately)
leave 10 mins, stir occasionally
  • turn heat down to 2, add chopped tomatoes

Flavour time

  • curry powder
  • garam masala
  • tumeric
  • paprika
  • cumin
  • coriander
  • chili
  • cinnamon
  • more pepper
  • salt
    •
leave 10 mins, lid on, heat 2/3
  • taste, add spices/salt/pepper depending on preference, stir
  • add spinach
  • stir in spinach
leave 5 mins
  • take off heat leave to cool uncovered

Done!
