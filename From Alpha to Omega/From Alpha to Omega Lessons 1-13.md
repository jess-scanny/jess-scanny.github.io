### 1st Declension: Types

>[!warning] The ε, ι, ρ Rule
>**IF**: the *stem* ends in **ε**, **ι**, or **ρ** 
>**THEN**: use **ᾱ** instead of **η** in the *singular* 

- **1st declension nouns:** 
	- Long vowel  (ᾱ/η)
	- Short vowel (α)
	- Masculine

### ω-Verbs: The Middle Voice
- The present and future use the **primary endings**
- The imperfect uses the **secondary endings**

| Primary Endings | Secondary Endings |
| --------------- | ----------------- |
| -ομαι           | -ομεν             |
| -ῃ/-ει          | -ου               |
| -εται           | -ετο              |
| -ομεθα          | -ομεθα            |
| -εσθε           | -εσθε             |
| -ονται          | -οντο             |
